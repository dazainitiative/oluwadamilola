Solution to the causes of Poverty

Conducive environment for learning

Daza Foundation seeks to provide teaching equipments to schools and facilities of learning to aid good and effective learning process.We believe that a child that is taught well in a good environment, with good teaching aid can assimilate well in class. Our vision is to see schools and educational sectors well equipped like other developed countries.With partnership of private individuals and companies we have a goal of providing teaching aid and equipments to less developed schools which must be achieved. We look further to having a better Educated Nigerian.

No to over-population

As we have seen the effects of over-population. Daza Foundation crew members are out on the quest to visit remote area to educate them about Family planning and why giving birth to many children can affect their lives and the country negatively, especially in this 21st century.We also hope to partner with more health firms to help facilitate knowledge.

Employment opportunities

Daza foundation has a group of trusted bodies who train people to be effective in their society.One of the means used is skill acquisition which is quite different from what you study in school.We train people to be Enterpreneurs in their society. Firstly,we teach people to see the problems around them, look for a solution to the problem and also create another job cycle for other people. Another means we use to create employment is to look out for job opportuniies in other firms and convey the message on our newsletter as soon as we get the information.the general public is advised to subscribe to the newsletter to get informations.Dont stay idle Nigeria!

Operation MR

Operation MR stands for Maintaining the available resources in the country and utilizing them well to serve as a source of livelihood. Over time Nigeria has been known to be a country of numerous resources but we lack the maintainenace culture.We dont manange our resources well and it waste or become a tapped material of foreign bodies.Daza Foundation is on the move to sensitize people on the need to appreciate what they have and make the best use of it.We hope to visit your community and help in the best way we can.

Operation GBF

Operation GBF means Go Back To Farm, with this initiative,food as a basic amenity of life will be solved. Daza Foundation,however,encourages gardens in every home where planting and cultivation can take place.Not to worry if you dont have a land, the scheme is for every one. We have bodies that lease their lands for cultivation for different year range.You dont need to pay,just work on the land and give a certain percentage of the cultivation to the land owners. All you need to do is to get across to us via our email,hotlines. We are just a call away to your doorstep.
