About Us

Daza Foundation is a non-governmental organization which seeks to put an end to basic lack and wants in the society. Human's basic needs are food,clothing and shelter. A man is said to be poor if he lacks all this resources,However, a Nation is poor if it can not provide these basic amenities for the citizens of that country.Lets solve the problem tagged "Poverty" by assisting people who are in need of help to achieve this basic needs of life.

For general information on our other products and services please contact our Customer Care Department within Lagos at (234) 80-837-71281, outside Lagos at (317) 572-3993 or fax (317) 572-4002.

What We Do

  • We empower people to be able to stand on their own as a self reliant citizen.
  • We educate people on vocational skills to help improve their creativity and have a source of livelihood.
  • We encourage agricultural system in all homes which create food for each family.
